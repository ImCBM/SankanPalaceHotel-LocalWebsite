Our Story

Founded in 2000, Sankan Palace Hotel has emerged as an emblem of luxury and exceptional hospitality in the industry. What began as a modest establishment has evolved into a renowned name synonymous with opulence and unparalleled service.

Over the years, we have continuously raised the bar in hospitality, innovating our services and amenities to meet the evolving needs of our discerning guests. Our journey has been marked by a steadfast commitment to excellence, which has earned us numerous accolades and a loyal clientele from around the world.

Today, Sankan Palace Hotel stands tall as a beacon of luxury hospitality, continuing to set new standards in the industry while honoring the rich traditions that have defined us from the beginning.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality services that exceed the expectations of our guests, creating memorable experiences through personalized service, luxurious accommodations, and culinary excellence. We strive to be the preferred choice for travelers seeking an unparalleled luxury experience.

Our Vision

To be globally recognized as the epitome of luxury hospitality, setting industry benchmarks for excellence, innovation, and sustainable practices. We aim to expand our presence while maintaining the highest standards of service and creating lasting value for our guests, employees, and stakeholders.
